About me

I am Mr. Susanta Roy, a dedicated and accomplished full-time Ph.D. Scholar with a strong background in Bioinformatics. I anticipate completing my Ph.D. in December 2023, having recently submitted my thesis in April 2023. My academic journey has been marked by a commitment to advancing our understanding of protein structural biology and drug target interaction analysis through molecular docking.

With a rich educational background, including an M.Sc. in Bioinformatics and a B.Sc. in Chemistry, Zoology, and Botany, I possess a well-rounded foundation in life sciences. My professional experience as an entrepreneur at Bio Explore and as a Bioinformatics Research Analyst at Arcgate has equipped me with a comprehensive skill set in the field. I have provided Bioinformatics services to international clients and conducted peptide characterization for a U.S.-based company. Additionally, my tenure as an Associate Bioinformatics Content Developer at the Bioinformatics Institute of India allowed me to engage in content writing and work as part of the Publication Management Team for "Bioinformatics Trends," the first Bioinformatics journal in India.

I hold an IBM Professional Certificate in Data Science, underscoring my commitment to professional development. My research has resulted in noteworthy publications, including the forthcoming "PhytoSelectDBT," a database for anti-diabetic targets docked with bioactive peptides, and "BioDADPep," a bioinformatics database for anti-diabetic peptides. My expertise extends to various areas, such as metagenomics, NGS data analysis, and the design of peptide biomarkers for diagnostics and therapeutics.

Operating seamlessly in both Windows and Ubuntu Linux environments, I am well-versed in data analysis and interpretation using command-line and GUI tools. As a dedicated educator, I have taught Bioinformatics in offline and online modes as part of my full-time Ph.D. My proficiency in web development, database creation, and text and web data mining further accentuates my technical capabilities.

My online presence includes profiles on LinkedIn and ResearchGate, and I am pleased to provide references from distinguished professionals who can vouch for my qualifications and work ethic.
